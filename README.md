## âš™ï¸ Functional Overview

### 1. EV Renter Features

- **Registration & Verification**
  - Create account and upload driving license + ID (CMND/CCCD)
  - Quick verification via station staff

- **Vehicle Booking**
  - Locate rental stations on map
  - View available vehicles (type, battery level, price)
  - Book in advance or on-site

- **Vehicle Pickup**
  - Check-in via app or counter
  - Sign digital or physical rental agreement
  - Confirm handover with staff (vehicle inspection, photo capture)

- **Vehicle Return**
  - Return to original station
  - Staff inspects and confirms vehicle condition
  - Pay any additional charges

- **Rental History & Insights**
  - View past rentals, costs, and distances
  - Analyze peak/off-peak rental patterns

---

### 2. Station Staff Features

- **Vehicle Handover Management**
  - View available, reserved, and rented vehicles
  - Perform handover procedures: inspect, photograph, update status
  - Co-sign digital confirmation with renter

- **Customer Verification**
  - Validate driving license and ID
  - Cross-check system records

- **On-site Payment Handling**
  - Record rental payments
  - Manage deposits and refunds

- **Station Vehicle Management**
  - Update battery and technical status
  - Report issues or damages to admin

---

### 3. Admin Features

- **Fleet & Station Oversight**
  - Monitor vehicle inventory per station
  - Track handover history and vehicle condition
  - Reallocate vehicles/staff during high demand

- **Customer Management**
  - Access customer profiles and rental history
  - Handle complaints and flag risky users

- **Staff Management**
  - Maintain staff directory
  - Evaluate performance (handover count, customer satisfaction)

- **Reporting & Analytics**
  - Revenue per station
  - Vehicle utilization rates and peak hours
